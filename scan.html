High Risk Interaction

Paracetamol + Alcohol

Regular alcohol consumption with paracetamol may increase risk of liver damage. Avoid excessive alcohol while taking this medication.

Moderate Risk Interaction

Paracetamol + Warfarin

Long-term use of paracetamol may increase the anticoagulant effect of warfarin. Monitor INR more frequently if used together.

Low Risk Interaction

Paracetamol + Ibuprofen

These can be used together safely for short-term pain relief. Alternate dosing may provide better continuous pain control.
